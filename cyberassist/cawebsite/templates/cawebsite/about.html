About Us

Cyber Assist is a 501(c)(3)-pending non-profit organization dedicated to helping those who are separated from or unfamiliar with the internet, making them unable to connect to the rest of the world and vulnerable to online scams and cyber attacks. By recycling and donating electronics to those in need, teaching children and senior citizens about technology and cybersecurity, and building software focused on teaching people about staying safe on the internet, we plan to bridge the gap between these two sectors of our world.

Because this problem is often overlooked, there aren’t many good solutions out there right now. Some people are simply unable to afford these devices, while other adults can afford the devices but simply can’t figure out how to use them because they never grew up these devices.

This problem is becoming more and more severe, while the benefits of technology only increase.
